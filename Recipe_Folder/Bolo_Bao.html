Bolo Bao

Hong Kong Pineapple Bun

Dough Ingredients

3.5 cups All-Purpose Flour
7 tbsp all-purpose flour (cake flour)
1 tbsp cornstarch (cake flour)
2.25 tsp Yeast
1.5 tsp Kosher Salt
1/3 cup sugar
2/3 cups heavy cream
1 egg
1 cup room temp milk
4 tbsp butter

Topping Ingredients

1/4 cup dry milk powder
1.25 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
2/3 cups superfine sugar
4 tbsp butter
2 tbps milk
1 egg yolk
1/8 tsp vanilla extract

Finishings

1 egg yolk

Making the dough

1. Start by mixing the heavy cream, milk, egg, sugar, cake flour, bread flour, yeast, and salt (in that order). Mix for 15 minutes.
2. Proof the dough for an hour in a warm area.
3. Mix for another 5 minutes to get rid of air bubbles. Cut the dough into equal pieces and place on a baking sheet.
4. Cover the buns and let them rise for another hour.

Making the topping

1. Mix dry milk powder, flour, baking soda, and superfine sugar. Make sure there are no lumps.
2. Add butter, milk, egg yolk, and vanilla. Mix everything until it becomes a dough.
3. Separate your topping into equal pieces and flatten into a circle.
4. Place each circle on top of a bun.

Baking the buns

1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Brush the top of each bun with egg yolk and bake for 12-13 minutes.

Bolo Bao

Hong Kong Pineapple Bun

Tangzhong Ingredients

2 tbsp all-purpose Flour
1/3 cup water

Dough Ingredients

4 cups Bread Flour
2.25 tsp Yeast
1 tsp Kosher Salt
1.5 tbsp sugar
4 tbsp condensed milk (or 2 tbsp milk powder + 3 tbsp sugar)
1 egg
0.625 cup room temp milk
4 tbsp butter

Topping Ingredients

1 tbsp room temp milk
1 cups all-purpose flour
2/3 cups superfine sugar
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 egg yolk
4 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/8 tsp turmeric (for color)

Egg Wash Ingredients

1 egg yolk
1 tbsp milk

Making the tangzhong

1. Whisk the water and flour together over heat until the flour dissolves.
2. After the mixture has thickened, pour into a bowl and place to the side to cool. This will thicken further while it cools.

Prepping the yeast

1. Microwave milk for 40 seconds to get it up to 110F-120F.
2. Add sugar and yeast and stir until dissolved.
3. Let this sit for 5-8 minutes. The foam should start forming because of the yeast.

Making the buns

1. Combine bread flour, salt, condensed milk, egg, melted butter, tangzhong, and foamy yeast mixture.
2. Mix everything together until the dough isn't sticky.
3. After the dough stops being sticky, take out the dough and fold it a few times.
4. Shape the dough into a ball and place back into the mixing bowl. Cover for an hour so that the buns can rise.

Making the topping

1. Place room-temp butter in a new bowl and beat until mashed.
2. Add the superfine sugar in two batches and mix into the butter. This mixture should by crumbly and should form a cream.
3. Add the egg yolk, milk, and vanilla extract and mix until combined.
4. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and tumeric.
5. Slowly mix the dry ingredients to the topping dough until fully mixed.
6. Place this in a different bowl to proof until the buns are done proofing.

Making the Bolo Bao

1. Divide the bun dough into small, equal spheres.
2. Prepare the egg wash by whisking the egg yolk and milk together.
3. Divid the topping dough into equal spheres and flatten until the bun and the topping have roughly the same circumference.
4. Gently place the topping on top of the bun.
5. Brush the toppings with egg wash.
6. Bake the buns at 375F for 14-16 minutes, or until the tops are golden.

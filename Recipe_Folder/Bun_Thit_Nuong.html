Bun Thit Nuong

Vietnamese Vermicelli Noodle Bowl

Ingredients

Vermicelli Noodles
Carne Asada/Pork Tenderloin/Chicken thigh
Onion
Green Onions
Bean Sprouts
Mushroom
Bell Pepper
Chile Peppers
Lime
Toasted Sesame Seeds

Meat Marinade

Brown Sugar (if using pork)
Fish Sauce
Light Soy Sauce
Rice Vinegar
Soy Sauce
Garlic
Sesame Oil
1 Lime

Instructions

1. Marinate the meat overnight.
2. Soak the noodles in hot water for 5-10 minutes. Vermicelli will absorb a lot of water so add a lot of water. Don't overcook or it'll break apart.
3. Heat up a pan and throw in the meat.
4. When the meat is browned, add the vegetables and noodles.
5. Sear the meat and add the vegetables and rice noodles. Stir it around so that everything is covered with the sauce.
6. Stir it a few times to ensure total coverage and turn off the heat when the dish is almost cooked.
7. Finish with toasted sesame seeds.

Notes

2/26/2026: The vermicelli absorbed a lot more water than I expected and I overcooked it. Next time, add excess water and cook for a shorter time. It will finish cooking in the pan. Meat marinade tasted good.

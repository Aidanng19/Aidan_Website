Braised Pork Belly

Chinese pork belly

Ingredients

Pork Belly
Carrots
Potatoes
Onions
Hard boiled eggs

Soup Base

Rock Sugar
Butter

Soup Base

Light Soy Sauce
Dark Soy Sauce
Shaoxing Wine
Mirin
Five Spice
Salt
Black Pepper
Star Anise
Bay Leaves
Ginger
Scallions
Water

Instructions

1. Cut the pork belly into medium sized pieces.
2. Parboil the pork belly to draw out impurities, then rince under cold water.
3. Set the pressure cooker to "saute" and caramelize the sugar and butter before dropping in the pork belly. Stir the pork belly to cover every surface with sugar. This will give the pork belly its color.
4. Add the soup base to the pressure cooker. Add enough liquid to almost cover all the ingredients. Mix the ingredients so they are alternating and the pork belly isn't stuck at the bottom. Don't add too much water or it'll dilute the sauce.
5. Cook for 30 minutes on high pressure and vent naturally.
6. Add peeled hard boiled eggs to the liquid and let it marinate for 12-24 hours or as long as I want. 6. After removing the solid ingredients, I can freeze and reuse the sauce if I feel like it. Otherwise, reduce the liquid so that it becomes a sauce and pour it over my dish.

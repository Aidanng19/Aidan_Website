Beef Oxtail Soup

Soup

Ingredients

Chicken Thighs

Marinade Seasonings

Salt
Black Pepper
Garlic Powder\Garlic Cloves
Gochugaru
Gochujang
Shaoxing Wine
Dark Soy Sauce
Sesame Oil

Instructions

1. Mix marinade seasonings and place the sliced chicken thigh in the marinade.
2. Let the chicken marinade for 12-24 hours.
3. Place chicken on aluminum foil and on a baking tray. Or stir fry it.
4. Air fry thicken at 425F for 15 minutes in toaster oven. Or until cooked in the wok.

Beef Chow Fun

Cantonese beef stir fry

Ingredients

1 pack Sa Ho Fun (Flat rice noodles)
Flank/Skirt Steak or Carne Asada
Onion
Green Onions
Bean Sprouts
Mushroom
Bell Pepper
Chile Peppers

Beef Marinade

1 tsp Baking Soda
1 tbsp Cornstarch
1 tbsp Oyster Sauce
1 tbsp Soy Sauce
1 tbsp Tamari Soy Sauce
White Pepper
1 tsp Cooking Wine
1 tsp Sugar
1 tbsp Sesame Oil

Instructions

1. Mix the marinade ingredients with the beef and let it sit for 24 hours.
2. Use your hands to loosen the rice noodles. Otherwise, it'll clump up while cooking.
3. Julienne the vegetables so they stir fry quickly.
4. Add some oil to the pan and add the beef with the marinade.
5. Sear the meat and add the vegetables and rice noodles. Stir it around so that everything is covered with the sauce.
6. Stir it a few times to ensure total coverage and turn off the heat when the dish is almost cooked.

Notes

2/25/2026: My stainless steel pan wasn't at the right temperature, so the noodles stuck to the pan. I didn't loosen them so they clumped up the entire time. The carne asada meat was very tender and I'm never using milanesa ever again. The vegetables cooked well. Next time, I need to loosen the rice noodles pre-emtively.

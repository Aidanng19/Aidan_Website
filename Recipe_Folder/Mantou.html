Mantou

Bread

Ingredients

3.75 cups bread flour
4 tbsp sugar
1 cup warm water
2.25 tsp yeast
1.5 tbsp vegetable oil
2 tsp salt

Instructions

1. Mix the dry ingredients. Pour in warm water and mix.
2. Knead until the dough starts forming and add the vegetable oil.
3. Keep kneading until the dough ball forms.
4. Let the dough proof for 12 hours in a warm area.
5. Roll the dough into a long log and cut into equal sized pieces.
6. Form each piece into a ball and place in the steamer to proof for an hour.
7. Steam them on high heat for 15 minutes.
8. Let the residue heat finish cooking them and help them keep their shape.

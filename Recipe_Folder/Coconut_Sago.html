Mango Sago

Dessert

Ingredients

6 cups water
1/3 cup small tapioca pearls
13.5 oz full-fat coconut milk
2 tbsp honey/maple syrup
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/8 tsp kosher salt
1/4 cup unsweetened coconut flakes

Instructions

1. Bring the water to a roiling boil.
2. When the water is boiling, pour in the tapioca.
3. Lower the heat and simmer the tapioca for 15-20 minutes or until the pearls are translucent. Stir to prevent sticking.
4. Pour out the hot water and pour cold water over them to prevent the tapioca from sticking.
5. Pour coconut milk, honey, vanilla, and salt into the empty saucepan.
6. Bring the coconut milk to a boil and stir to dissolve the additives.
7. Turn off the heat and stir in the tapioca.
8. Transfer to a sealed container and chill in the fridge for 2 hours.
9. When ready to serve, take out the sago and stir to mix up the fat on the surface.
10. Add the shaved coconut and mango and serve.

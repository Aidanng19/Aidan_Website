Brazo De Mercedes

Filipino log cake

Ingredients

10 egg whites
10 egg yolks
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp cream of tartar
3/4 cup granulated sugar
14 oz condensed milk

Baking the meringue

1. Combine egg whites and cream of tartar and beat until soft peaks form.
2. Slowly add the sugar while mixing until the texture is semi-firm to form the meringue.
3. Place the meringue on top of parchment paper. Thie should be roughly an inch thick.
4. Bake at 350F for 20 minutes or until the top of the meringue turns medium brown.

Making the filling

1. Beat the egg yolks until homogenous and add condensed milk in a small pot.
2. Apply low heat and cook the mixture while stirring until the texture becomes thick.
3. Add vanilla extract and take this off the heat. Stir until blended.

Combining the meringue and filling

1. Flip the meringue and spread the filling over the meringue.
2. Roll the meringue until it forms a log.

Ginger Milk Pudding

Dessert

Ingredients

1 cup whole milk
1-2 tbsp ginger juice

Optional

Sugar
Honey

Instructions

1. Peel and grate ginger, then push through a sieve to extract ginger juice.
2. Heat the milk til hot, but not boiling (170F).
2a. Optional: Add sugar to the milk while it's heating.
3. Add ginger juice in a bowl, then pour in the milk from a height so that they mix.
4. Let sit for 10-15 minutes to set.
4a. Optional: Top the pudding with honey.

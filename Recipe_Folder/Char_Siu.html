Char Siu

Pork

Ingredients

Pork Shoulder

Marinade

Salt
Garlic Powder
Five Spice Powder
Shaoxing Wine
Brown Sugar
Light Soy Sauce
Oyster Sauce
Hoisin Sauce
Fermented Bean Curd

Instructions

1. Poke the pork with a fork to help the marinade get deeper into the meat.
2. Mix the marinade and massage it into the pork.
3. Let the pork marinate for 6-8 hours in the refridgerator.
4. Let the pork warm up to room temperature after removing from the refridgerator.
5. Preheat the oven to 425F.
6. Cook for 15 minutes, then baste both sides with the marinade. Cook for another 15 minutes.
7. Baste with water and honey mixture. Then cook for another 5 minutes.
8. Let the char siu rest before cutting into it.

Notes

1/24/2026: The surface was a little burnt, but the meat is very tender. The seasoning is really strong and this tastes really good. Next time, adjust the marinade to be less watery. The restaurants' fall apart a little more, so maybe turn down the temp to slow cook?

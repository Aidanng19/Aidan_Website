Japanese Chicken Curry

Chicken

Ingredients

Sliced Boneless Chicken
Onion
Garlic
Potatoes
Carrots
Ginger
Japanese Curry Roux

Dry Seasonings

Salt
Black Pepper
White Pepper
1 tbsp butter

Instructions

1. Slice the onions thinly, mince the garlic/ginger, roll cut the carrots/potatoes, slice the chicken.
2. Optional: Season the chicken with salt and pepper overnight.
3. Melt the butter in the pan.
4. Caramelize the onions, garlic, ginger until brown.
5. Cook the chicken until the surface is cooked.
6. Break up 1 roux of curry and add water. Mix everything until the curry dissolves. Simmer for 10 minutes until everything is mixed.
7. Add more salt, black/white pepper to make the curry sharper.
8. Let the liquid reduce and pour over rice.

Notes

1/29/2026: This tastes pretty good, but a bit weak. I didn't reduce the sauce enough or I should've only filled half the pan with water. The curry I had in Japan had a lot more black/white pepper and had a stronger taste. I will add more salt and pepper next time to the sauce.

## Ingredients
Boneless Chicken Thighs\
Onions\
Carrots\
Potatoes\
Ginger\
Garlic\

# Sauce
Olive Oil\
Light Soy Sauce\
1 block curry roux\

# Seasonings
Salt\
Black Pepper\

## Instructions
1. Cut onions into slices. Cut carrots into rolling wedges. Quarter and rinse potatoes. Cut the chicken thighs into small chunks. 
2. Cook the onions and ginger in a pan until they're caramelized. Add the chicken and cook until the surface is browned. Add the seasonings here.
3. Add enough water to almost cover the surface of the chicken.
4. Add the soy sauce, carrots, and potatoes.
5. Simmer the pot for 15 minutes until the potatoes are soft.
6. Add the curry cubes and stir until it is dissolved. 
7. Simmer again until everything is mixed well.

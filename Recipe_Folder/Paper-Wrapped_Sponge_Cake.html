Paper-Wrapped Sponge Cake

Chinese sponge cake

Egg Yolk Batter

5 Egg Yolks
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup milk
2 tbsp granulated sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
3/4 cup cake flour
1/4 tsp baking powder

Meringue

5 Egg Whites
1/2 cup cream of tartar
1/4 cup granulated sugar

Instructions

1. Whisk the egg yolks, vegetable oil, and milk until homogenous.
2. Whisk in sugar and vanilla extract.
3. Sift in the flour and baking powder. Keep whisking until smooth. Then set aside.
4. Beat the egg whites until frothy and add the cream of tartar.
5. Gradually add in the sugar while beating. Continue beating until stiff peaks.
6. Gently fold the egg whites into the egg yolks batter until combined.
7. Pour into baking sheet cups evenly.
8. Bake at 350F for 20-25 minutes.

Miso Soup

Tofu

Ingredients

1 tbsp miso:1 cup water
1 piece Kombu (Dried kelp)/Dashi Powder
1 cup katsuobushi (dried bonito flakes)
Silken Tofu
Dried wakame seaweed
Green Onion/Scallion

Instructions

1. Soak the kombu in water from 1 hour to 12 hours to extract the umami from the kombu and create dashi stock before adding miso. Remove the kombu after this is done.
1a. Steep the kombu powder in hot water for 5 minutes.
2. Add miso to the stock and stir it frequently to prevent the miso from clumping up.
3. Add the tofu, katsuobushi, and seaweed as the soup is simmering.
4. Reduce the heat before the miso reaches a full boil and let it sit for >5 minutes.
5. If the miso needs to be reheated, reheat it slowly without boiling it or it'll lose its flavor.

Milk Custard Cubes

Dessert

Ingredients

2 egg yolks
3 tbsp sugar
1.5 cup milk
1/4 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup + 2 tbsp cornstarch

Egg Wash

1 egg yolk

Instructions

1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar together.
2. Add the milk once homogenous.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk the heavy cream and cornstarch together.
4. Combine the two bowls.
5. Cook this mixture on medium heat until it has the consistency of a pudding.
6. Pour mixture into a class container and let it chill for 1 hour until solidified.
7. This should be bouncy and have texture like jello.
8. Cut the jello into equal pieces and brush the surface with egg wash. This egg wash will caramelize while baking. 9. Bake at 350F for 15-20 minutes or until the tops are browned.

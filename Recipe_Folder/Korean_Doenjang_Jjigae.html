Korean Doenjang Jjigae

Soup

Ingredients

Beef
Tofu

Soup base

Garlic
Green Chili
Green Onions
Sesame Oil
Doenjang/Miso Paste
Gochugaru
Fish Sauce

Instructions

1. Stir fry the doenjang, sesame oil, garlic, and gochugaru to release the flavor.
2. Add water for the soup and wait for it to boil.
3. Add in beef to start building flavor. 3. Add in fish oil, green chili, green onions, and tofu and wait until everything is cooked.

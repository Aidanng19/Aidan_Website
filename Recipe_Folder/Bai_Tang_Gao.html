Bai Tang Gao

Chinese Sponge Cake

Starter

2 tbsp warm water
1 tsp sugar
2 tsp instant yeast

Batter

2.5 cups rice flour
1.25 cups water

Syrup

0.875 cups granulated sugar
1.25 cups boiling water

Finisher

1 tsp baking powder

Prepare the starter

1. Put all ingredients in a bowl and mix. Let sit for 15 minutes until foamy.

Making the batter

1. Mix all the batter ingredients and set aside.

Making the syrup

1. Mix the syrup ingredients in a sauce pan and boil until the sugar dissolves.

Combining everything

1. Pour the hot syrup into the batter and mix until it cools to a comfortably warm temperature.
2. When it's cooled sufficiently, add the starter mixture so the yeast doesn't die from the heat.
3. Mix everything sufficiently. This mixture should be watery.
4. Let this proof in a warm location for an hour until bubbles form.

Steaming

1. Add water to the steamer and bring to a rolling boil.
2. Stir the batter to push out the bubbles and mix the baking powder into the mixture right before steaming.
3. Steam on medium for 30 minutes.
4. When finished, the top of the gao should be dry and a toothpick should be able to poke it cleanly.
5. Let this gao rest before cutting into it.

Pork Tenderloin

Pork

Ingredients

Pork Tenderloin

Dry Seasonings

Salt
Black Pepper
Paprika
Garlic Powder
Chili Powder
Italian Seasoning
Cumin

Instructions

1. Season the pork tenderloin on all sides using the dry seasonings for 12-24 hours. This will act as our dry brine.
2. Place the seasoned pork on aluminum foil and place it in the fridge.
3. Preheat the oven to 400F and cook for 12 minutes until the center reaches 145F.
4. Take out the pork and let it rest so the juices are sealed in.

Notes

2/3/2026: The pork was undercooked by a few minutes, so I bumped it up from 10 -> 12 minutes. I only let it dry brine for 10 hours, so I think it could've benefitted from a few more hours.

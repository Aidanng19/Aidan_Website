Pan Sobao

Puerto Rican Bread

Ingredients

3 cups All-Purpose Flour
2.25 tsp Yeast
3 tsp Kosher Salt
1.25 cups warm milk
1.25 cups White Sugar
1 egg
6 tbsp butter

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients with a large wooden spoon until mixed.
2. Mix the wet ingredients together and slowly mix into the dry ingredients.
3. Knead everything together until the dough is smooth and not sticky.
4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 12 hours in the refridgerator.
5. Let the bread warm up to room temperature before forming them into logs.
6. Bake at 350F until golden.

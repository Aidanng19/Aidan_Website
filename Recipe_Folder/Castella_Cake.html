Castella Cake

Japanese Rice Cooker Sponge Cake

Meringue

4 Egg Whites
1/4 cup Granulated Sugar

Batter

4 Egg Yolks
1/4 cup Granulated Sugar

Other Ingredients

0.75 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
3 tbsp milk
5 tbsp vegetable oil

Making the Meringue

1. To make the meringue, whip the egg whites. Slowly add the sugar until the meringue is firm and glossy.
2. To make the batter, beat the egg yolks. Slowly add the sugar until creamy.
3. Fold 1/3 of the meringue into the batter and mix until homogenous.
4. Mix the batter into the rest of the meringue and mix until homogenous.
5. Add the flour and baking powder and mix until homogenous.
6. Mix the milk and oil together. Add a little batter to this to emulsify it, then pour it into the rest of the batter and combine until completely folded.
7. Pour into your rice cooker and cook normally.

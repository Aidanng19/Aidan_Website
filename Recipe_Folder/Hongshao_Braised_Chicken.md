
## Ingredients
Boneless Chicken Thighs\
Ginger\
Garlic\

# Sauce
Olive Oil\
Light Soy Sauce\
Dark Soy Sauce\
Shaoxing wine\
Sesame oil\
Chili oil


# Seasonings
Salt\
Black Pepper\

## Instructions
1. Dry and season both sides of the chicken thigh with salt and pepper.
2. Pour olive oil into a pan and season with ginger and garlic.
3. Place the chicken in the pan and sear both sides.
4. Create a sauce using the light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, shaoxing wine, sesame oil, and chili oil, then pour into the pan, covering up to half of the chicken.
5. Boil the chicken for 10 minutes, flipping halfway to reduce the liquid and fully cook.

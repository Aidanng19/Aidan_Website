Green Onion Pancake

Chinese Pancake

Pancake Ingredients

3 cups all-purpose flour
3g salt
5g yeast powder
300ml warm water
1 tsp chopped green onion

Oil

Sesame Oil

Instructions

1. Mix the flour, salt, and yeast in a bowl.
2. Add the warm water and mix.
3. Add the green onions after the dough is almost formed and fold them in.
4. Roll the dough into a log and cut it into fist sized balls.
5. Flatten the balls and brush sesame oil on one side.
5. Make a cut along a radius and roll into a cone-shape.
6. Pinch the edges until it resembles a ball to seal the oil in. Then flatten it into a pancake again.
7. Pan-fry in an anti-stick pan until both sides are brown and the pancake starts inflating in the middle.

Leche Flan

Filipino custard dessert

Ingredients

3 Llanera molds
9 tbsp sugar
12 egg yolks
2 tbsp water
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

1. Place 3 tbsp of sugar into each of the 3 llanera molds.
2. Place llanera molds over the stovetop and use low heat to melt the sugar and water? until it coats the bottom of the mold.
3. Allow the llanera to cool until it forms a caramel crust.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks together.
5. Add condensed milk and mix until combined.
6. Add evaporated milk and vanilla extract, stir gently until blended.
7. Pour into the llanera molds and cover tightly with foil.
8. Bring the steamer to a boil, then lower the heat to a simmer.
9. Steam the llaneras for 30-35 minutes.
10. Flip the flan over and serve.

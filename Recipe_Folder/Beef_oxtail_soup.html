Beef Oxtail Soup

Soup

Ingredients

Beef Oxtail
Celery
Carrots
Potatoes
Onions

Soup Seasonings

Butter
Salt
Black Pepper
Garlic
Ginger
Star Anise
Peppercorn
Bay Leaves
Shaoxing Wine
Dark Soy Sauce

Instructions

1. Soak the beef oxtail in cold water for 12-24 hours to draw out the blood. Change the water a few times to keep the water clean. This will keep the broth clear.
2. Optional: Blanch the beef oxtail for 20 minutes in a pot to remove impurities.
3. Optional: Rinse the beef oxtail under cold water to wash off the scum.
4. Melt butter in the instant pot. Place the beef oxtail in the pressure cooker and sear the meat. Add salt and pepper.
5. Add peppercorn, star anise, ginger, garlic and toast them.
5. Add vegetables, bay leaves, shaoxing wine, dark soy sauce and water in the pressure cooker. Add enough water to cover the meat, but not the veggies. The fat will leech out of the oxtail and create soup, so you don't need stock.
6. Set the pressure cooker to high and let it cook for 30 minutes. Allow the pressure cooker to naturally release. This will take 1.5 hours.
7. Let cool and store in plastic containers.

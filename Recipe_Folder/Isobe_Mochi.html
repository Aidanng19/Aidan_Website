Isobe Mochi

Japanese tofu mochi

Ingredients

300g (1 pack) silken tofu
8 tbsp potato starch

Seasonings

3 tbsp sugar
3 tbsp soy sauce
3 tbsp mirin

Instructions

1. Place silken tofu in a bowl and beat with a fork until completely smooth.
2. Add the potato starch and mix until the mixture becomes thick and sticky.
3. Heat a drying pan and add a thin layer of oil.
4. Dollop the mixture onto the pan and fry both sides until golden.
5. Mix the sauce and pour into the pan. This will glaze the mochi on both sides.

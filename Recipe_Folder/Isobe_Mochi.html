Isobe Mochi

Japanese tofu mochi

Ingredients

300g (1 pack) silken tofu
8 tbsp potato starch

Seasonings

3 tbsp sugar
3 tbsp soy sauce
3 tbsp mirin

Instructions

1. Place silken tofu in a bowl and whisk until completely smooth.
2. Add the potato starch and mix until the mixture becomes thick and sticky.
3. Heat a drying pan and add a thin layer of oil.
4. Dollop the mixture onto the pan and fry both sides until golden.
5. Mix the sauce and pour into the pan. This will glaze the mochi on both sides.

Notes

03/04/2026: I added the potato starch too early and the tofu wasn't smooth and ended up being lumpy. I didn't add oil so it didn't cook correctly in my pan and ended up being a watery pancake.

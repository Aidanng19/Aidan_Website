Pork Adobo

Filipino Braised Pork

Ingredients

Pork Belly

Aromatics

Ginger
Garlic
Onion

Marinade

Salt
Peppercorn/Black Pepper
Bay Leaf
Light Soy Sauce
Vinegar
Water

Instructions

1. Cut pork belly into 2 inch chunks.
2. Dice the ginger, garlic, and onion.
3. Put some oil in the pan and saute the pork belly until the surface is brown everywhere.
4. While saute-ing, the fat from the pork belly will render. Remove the pork from the pan and add the ginger, garlic, and onion. Saute until brown.
5. Add the pork belly back into the pan and add the salt, peppercorn, bay leaves, soy sauce, vinegar and water. Add enough liquid to almost cover the top of the pork belly.
6. Simmer for one hour.
7. Add a little brown sugar at the end to reduce the taste of the acidity.

Notes

03/06/2026: The meat had a weird aftertaste, I wonder if it's because of the impurities in the pork. Everything else tasted good. There was a lot of oil, so maybe I should add less vegetable oil at the start so the fat can render more.

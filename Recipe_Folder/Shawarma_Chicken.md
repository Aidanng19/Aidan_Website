## Ingredients
Boneless Chicken Thighs

# Marinade Seasonings
Salt\
Black Pepper\
Cumin\
Paprika\
Cayenne Pepper\
Garlic Powder/Garlic Cloves\
Cardamom\
Coriander\
Lemon Juice\
Olive Oil


## Instructions
1. Mix marinade seasonings and place the sliced chicken thigh in the marinade.
2. Let the chicken marinade for 12-24 hours.
3. Place chicken on aluminum foil and on a baking tray.
4. Air fry thicken at 425F for 15 minutes in toaster oven.

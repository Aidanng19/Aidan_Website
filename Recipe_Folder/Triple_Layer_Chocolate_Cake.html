Triple Layer Chocolate Cake

Cake

Cake Ingredients

1.75 cups All-Purpose Flour
0.75 cups unsweetened natural cocoa powder
1.75 cups granulated sugar
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 tsp espresso powder
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 large eggs at room temperature
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup buttermilk at room temperature
1 cup brewed hot coffee

Frosting Ingredients

2.5 sticks butter
3.5 cups confectioners sugar
0.75 cups unsweetened natural cocoa powder
3 tbsp heavy cream at room temperature
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla extract

Prepare the cake

1. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, salt and espresso powder.
2. In a separate bowl, mix the oil, eggs, and vanilla extract together until combined.
3. Once mixed, add the buttermilk and mix until combined.
4. Mix the dry and wet bowl, add the hot coffee and whisk until everything is fully mixed. The batter will be thin and watery.
5. Pour the batter into the mold of your desired shape. Bake for 25 minutes or until the batter is cooked.
6. Remove the batter from the mold and place on a wire rack to cool.

Making the frosting

1. Using a paddle, beat the butter until creamy.
2. Add confectioners sugar, cocoa powder, 3 tbsp heavy cream, salt, and vanilla extract.
3. Beat slowly for 30 seconds until everything is somewhat mixed, then beat at high speed for a minute until everything is a cream.
3a. Add more confectioners sugar and cocoa powder if the frosting is too thin and add more heavy cream if it's too thick.

Assembling the cake

1. Place one layer on a flat surface and evenly cover the top with frosting. Repeat for as many layers as you have
2. Finish by covering the surface and sides with frosting.
3. Refridgerate the cake for at least 1 hour before cutting into it to help it maintain its shape.

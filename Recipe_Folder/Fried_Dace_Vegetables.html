Fried Dace Vegetables

Fried Dace with Salted Black Beans Stir Fry

Ingredients

1 Can of Fried Dace with Salted Black Beans
Garlic
A Choy
Chrysanthemum Greens

Instructions

1. Dump the can of fried dace into the pan. This will have enough oil that you don't need additional oil.
2. Stir fry the garlic with the dace until fragrant.
3. Add the washed vegetables and stir fry on high heat until cooked.
4. Add any additional seasonings as desired.

Notes

2/15/2026: The dace oil had a very strong flavor to it that tasted really good. This is typically described as the "umami" flavor. I added oyster sauce and hoisin sauce, but I probably didn't need to. I think this dish is good as it is.
